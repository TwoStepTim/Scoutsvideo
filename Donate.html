Support Our Scouts
Your Donation Makes a Difference

Supporting the scouts of 253 means more than just financial assistance; it's about fostering a sense of community, leadership, and personal growth. Your donations directly impact our scouts' ability to participate in enriching activities, acquire necessary equipment, and engage in meaningful community service projects. With your support, we can continue to empower these young individuals to become the leaders of tomorrow.


Mailing Of Checks

If you prefer to make a donation via check, you can send it to the Watts Chapel address at 3703 Tryon Rd, Raleigh, NC 27606, with attention to the scouts of 253. Your contribution will be gratefully received and put towards furthering the scouting experience for our dedicated group of young individuals. Thank you for your support in shaping a brighter future for our scouts.
