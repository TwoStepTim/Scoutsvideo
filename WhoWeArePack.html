ABOUT PACK 253

About Us

Pack 253, chartered by Watts Chapel Missionary Baptist Church in the Occoneechee Council, is dedicated to fostering the growth and development of young boys and girls through the values-based Cub Scouting program. Our pack provides a nurturing environment where children from kindergarten to fifth grade can explore new adventures, develop leadership skills, and build lasting friendships.

At Pack 253, we believe in the power of Scouting to shape the future leaders of our community. Our mission is to offer a program that emphasizes personal fitness, character development, and citizenship responsibilities, all while ensuring that our Cubs have fun and create cherished memories.

Our Organization's Charter

Watts Chapel Missionary Baptist Church plays a vital role in supporting Pack 253, providing a foundation for our activities and ensuring that our program thrives. With the church's support, we can deliver an enriching Scouting experience that impacts our children's lives positively.

Chartering Organization Support

The Boy Scouts of America extends the reach of Scouting to young people across the nation by partnering with community organizations to establish, organize, and oversee Cub Scout packs, Boy Scout troops, Varsity Scout teams, Venturing crews, and Sea Scout ships for boys and girls up to age 18. These partnering organizations assume responsibility for managing the units and shaping the program of activities in alignment with their specific goals and objectives. Chartered Organization Representative (COR)

The Chartered Organization Representative (COR) serves as the crucial link between Pack 253 and our chartering organization. This role involves:

  • Ensuring our pack operates smoothly and in alignment with both BSA standards and our charter organization's values.
  • Serving as a voting member of the BSA council and the district committee, representing our pack's interests.
  • Acting as a liaison, sharing updates and information between the pack, district, and council, ensuring our Cubs have access to the best resources and opportunities.

What We Do

In Pack 253, our Cubs engage in a variety of fun and educational activities, including:

  • Outdoor Adventures: Camping trips, nature hikes, and outdoor skills training.
  • Community Service: Participating in local service projects to give back to our community.
  • Skill Building: Learning valuable life skills through hands-on activities and badge achievements.
  • Teamwork and LeadershipDeveloping teamwork and leadership skills through group activities and pack events.

ON OUR HONOR

Scout Oath

On my honor I will do my best to do my duty to God and my country and to obey the Scout Law; To help other people at all times; To keep myself physically strong, mentally awake, and morally straight.

Outdoor Code

As an American, I will do my best to be clean in my outdoor manners, be careful with fire, be considerate in the outdoors and to be conservation-minded.

Scout Law

A Scout is trustworthy, loyal, helpful, friendly, courteous, kind, obedient, cheerful, thrifty, brave, clean, and reverent.
