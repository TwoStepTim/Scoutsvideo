253 Outdoor Adventures
Exciting Expeditions Await!

Embark on thrilling outdoor adventures with 253! Our scouts is dedicated to providing unforgettable experiences immersed in the beauty of nature. From exhilarating hikes through scenic trails to campouts under the starlit sky, we offer a wide range of activities that ignite the spirit of exploration and camaraderie among scouts.

253's outdoor adventures are designed to foster teamwork, resilience, and a deep appreciation for the great outdoors. Whether sliding down snow mountain, navigating pristine waterways, or mastering essential survival skills, scouts are empowered to challenge themselves and create lasting memories in nature's playground. Join us as we embark on exciting expeditions that inspire growth, friendship, and a lifelong love for adventure!

253 Community Engagement
Making a Difference Together

253 takes pride in its active involvement within the community, believing that true leadership extends beyond outdoor adventures to making a meaningful difference in the lives of others. Our scouts engage in a variety of community service projects, from environmental conservation efforts to supporting local charities and organizing events that bring people together. These activities not only contribute positively to our surroundings but also instill a sense of responsibility and compassion in our members.

Through 253's community engagement initiatives, scouts learn the importance of teamwork, empathy, and civic duty. By working closely with local organizations and participating in service-oriented missions, we aim to create a lasting impact on our community while fostering the development of our scouts into conscientious and caring leaders. Join us in our mission to serve, uplift, and unite, as we build stronger communities and forge bonds that will last a lifetime.

Scout Oath

On my honor I will do my best To do my duty to God and my country and to obey the Scout Law; To help other people at all times; To keep myself physically strong, mentally awake, and morally straight.

The Values of Scouting

Scouting promotes active participation and hands-on learning, fostering growth and community engagement for both Cub Scouts and Boy Scouts through regular meetings and adventurous activities. This approach instills lifelong values and skills in every scout.

View details »

Scout Law

A Scout is trustworthy, loyal, helpful, friendly, courteous, kind, obedient, cheerful, thrifty, brave, clean, and reverent.
